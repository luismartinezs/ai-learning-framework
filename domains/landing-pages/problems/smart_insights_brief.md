## Product Description

Smart Insights is a digital marketing education platform offering guides, templates, e-books, and training modules covering the full spectrum of digital marketing disciplines. The platform is led by Dr. Dave Chaffey, recognized by the Chartered Institute of Marketing as one of 50 people who shaped the future of marketing. The content is practical and action-oriented, designed for professionals applying it to real campaigns. Revenue model is subscription-based paid membership.

## Target Audience

Digital marketing professionals who need structured, expert-level guidance on strategy and execution. Two distinct segments: (1) longtime followers of Dr. Chaffey who already trust the brand and are considering paid membership, and (2) newcomers discovering the platform for the first time who have no context on the depth of resources available. The newcomer segment is the primary underserved group. These are working professionals, not students. They need answers they can apply to active campaigns.

## Primary Problem Solved

Digital marketing is broad and constantly changing. Professionals struggle to keep up across SEO, paid media, email, social, analytics, and strategy. Free content (blogs, podcasts, YouTube) is abundant but fragmented, inconsistent in quality, and lacks the structured depth needed to make confident decisions. Hiring a consultant to fill knowledge gaps is expensive and episodic.

## Key Mechanism

Aggregates expert-curated, structured digital marketing education into a single subscription. Instead of assembling guidance from scattered free sources or paying for one-off consulting engagements, members get ongoing access to a comprehensive library maintained by recognized industry authorities. The value proposition is expert consulting depth at subscription pricing.

## Constraints

- Subscription pricing model (competes against abundant free marketing content online)
- The founder's personal brand is a major trust asset but also means prospects who don't know him see less immediate credibility
- The platform's resource library is extensive, but prospects don't grasp the breadth before signing up
- Content is primarily for the UK/international English-speaking market
- Has worked with well-known publications (GQ, New Yorker, WIRED, Glamour), which is a credibility asset
